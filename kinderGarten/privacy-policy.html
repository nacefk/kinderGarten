Sghiri - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Sghiri ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Sghiri mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username and authentication credentials provided by the kindergarten administration.
  • Child Information: Name, date of birth, gender, class assignment, health information (allergies, medical notes), emergency contacts, and authorized pickup persons — as entered by parents or administrators.
  • Photos: Profile photos taken via camera or selected from gallery for child and user profiles.
  • Attendance Data: Daily attendance records and absence reports.
  • Activity Reports: Daily reports including meals, behavior, naps, and notes.
  • Messages: Chat messages exchanged between parents and administrators.

2. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera to allow you to:

  • Take profile photos for children's profiles.
  • Capture photos for activity documentation.

Photos are uploaded securely to our servers and are only accessible to authorized users (parents and administrators) of the same kindergarten.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Manage kindergarten operations (attendance, activities, planning).
  • Enable communication between parents and kindergarten staff.
  • Provide parents with daily reports and updates about their children.
  • Display profile photos within the application.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared:

  • Within your kindergarten's authorized users (parents see only their own children's data; administrators see data for children in their kindergarten).
  • With service providers who assist in operating our application (hosting, push notifications), under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting your kindergarten administrator.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for use by parents and kindergarten staff to manage childcare. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children. All child-related information is entered by parents or authorized administrators.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact your kindergarten administrator.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
